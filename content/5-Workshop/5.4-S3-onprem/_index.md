#### Objectives

After implementing the upload workflow, the next step is to verify that the entire process works correctly and that documents are stored successfully.

#### Implementation Steps

**Step 1:** Open the CloudDoc interface and select a document to upload.

![Upload a document](/images/5-Workshop/5.4-S3-onprem/anh1.png)

**Step 2:** Monitor the upload progress and verify that the process completes without issues.

![Monitor the upload process](/images/5-Workshop/5.4-S3-onprem/anh2.png)

**Step 3:** Verify the final result to confirm that the document has been received successfully by the system.

![Upload result](/images/5-Workshop/5.4-S3-onprem/anh3.png)

{{% notice info %}}
When all steps are completed successfully, the uploaded document is ready to enter the moderation workflow.
{{% /notice %}}

#### Expected Results

The document is uploaded successfully, all required information is recorded, and the system is ready for the remaining processing steps.
