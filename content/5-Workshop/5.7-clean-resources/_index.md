#### Objectives

After completing the workshop, unused resources should be cleaned up to avoid unnecessary costs and prepare the environment for future deployments.

#### Cleanup Steps

**Step 1:** Remove temporary data stored in the Amazon S3 bucket.

![Remove Objects from S3](/images/5-Workshop/5.7-clean-resources/anh1.png)

**Step 2:** Stop or terminate the EC2 instance used during testing.

![Terminate EC2 Instance](/images/5-Workshop/5.7-clean-resources/anh2.png)

**Step 3:** Review monitoring and alarm configurations before shutting down the environment.

**Step 4:** Verify that no remaining resources are left behind.

#### Completion

After the cleanup process is finished, the testing environment has been released successfully and is ready for future deployments or practice sessions.
